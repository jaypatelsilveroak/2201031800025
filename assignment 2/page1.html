Building Bridges to Mental Health

Experience compassionate care from the comfort of your own space with telepsych

Compassionate care is at the forefront of our services.

Our experienced providers specialize in evaluation and medication management of a range of mental health conditions. We focus on adults aged 18 and above, offering expertise in addressing depression, bipolar disorders, anxiety disorders, PTSD, dementia, psychotic disorders, insomnia, and smoking cessation. Our dedicated team is committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to support your mental well-being.

*Please note that while we are dedicated to addressing various mental health concerns, we do not specialize in the treatment of ADHD or routinely prescribe controlled substances such as Adderall and Xanax.*

Providers You Can Trust

Mind and Body Wellness delivers highly competent and individualized mental health care by providing support systems and building trusting relationships that promote empowerment, healing, and improved quality of life.

Ask about our house call program

We bring mental health care directly to the doorsteps of seniors and individuals who are homebound. Our dedicated team of professionals understand the unique challenges faced by those who may find it difficult to access traditional clinic settings. We are committed to providing quality mental health services tailored to meet the individual needs of each client in the comfort and familiarity of their own homes. Call today to determine if you or your loved one are eligible for our in-home mental health services.
