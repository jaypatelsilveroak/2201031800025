Services

Medication Management

Our providers prescribe and oversee mental health medications to ensure they are taken properly and achieving their therapeutic outcome as planned. This process includes initial and ongoing medication review to address safety and adherence concerns, reduce adverse drug events, and provide education.

Access to Counseling

Mind and Body Wellness facilitates access to psychotherapy and counseling by licensed clinical professionals. Request a referral during your appointment.

Coordination of Care

Mind and Body Wellness works together with a network of health care professionals to include primary care physicians, pain management specialists, podiatrists, physical and occupational therapists, and nurses to provide safe and comprehensive care for individuals that participate in our house call program.
