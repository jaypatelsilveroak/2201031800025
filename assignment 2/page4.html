Meet the Team

Talia Nelson, MSN, FNP-C, PMHNP-BC

Talia Nelson is a highly accomplished dual-certified Family and Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner, bringing a wealth of expertise gained through extensive training and hands-on experience in diverse healthcare settings. Her proficiency spans both psychiatric and medical conditions, with a versatile background ranging from hospital and rehabilitation environments to roles as an educator.

Specializing in the treatment of Depression, Anxiety, Dementia, Bipolar disorder, Schizophrenia, Insomnia, and Smoking Cessation for Adults and Geriatrics. Talia employs a comprehensive approach rooted in evidence-based guidelines. Her practice encompasses medication management, talk therapy, and a commitment to promoting natural treatments such as vitamins, supplements, lifestyle modification, diet, and exercise.

Talia's clinical acumen is further augmented by her innate ability to establish meaningful connections with patients and their families. Devoted to delivering individualized and comprehensive patient care, she prioritizes the well-being of those under her care.

Commencing her medical career in the Military as a combat medic, Talia's dedication to excellence led her to pursue continuous education. In 1998, she achieved her first nursing certification, subsequently earning an Associate degree in Nursing from Excelsior College. Building on her foundation, she obtained a master's degree as a Family Nurse Practitioner and a Post-Masters Certification as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner from Walden University and Northern Kentucky University, respectively.

For Talia, her work is not merely a profession but a calling in life. Her commitment to helping others is evident in her unwavering dedication to patient care. Beyond her professional pursuits, Talia finds joy in spending quality time with family and friends, exploring her culinary interests through cooking, indulging in travel, and savoring diverse cuisines.
